**üìå Project Overview**

Customer churn is one of the biggest revenue risks for subscription-based businesses. Losing a customer not only impacts immediate revenue but also reduces lifetime value, increases acquisition costs, and weakens long-term growth.

This project approaches churn as a forward-looking business problem, not just a historical metric.

I built an end-to-end churn analytics solution that combines:

-> Churn probability predictions

-> Customer behavior analysis

-> Revenue impact quantification

-> Actionable retention insights

The final output is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to help business, marketing, and customer success teams identify at-risk customers early and act before churn occurs.

üéØ Business Problem

The business operates on a B2C subscription model and has observed:

-> Rising churn within the first 30 days of subscription

-> Revenue leakage due to early customer exits

-> Limited visibility into who is likely to churn and why

Key Challenges:

-> Churn reporting is reactive, not predictive

-> Churn probabilities exist but are not actionable

-> No clear view of revenue at risk

-> Retention teams lack prioritization

üß† Project Objectives

This project aims to:

-> Predict customer churn risk using behavioral signals

-> Segment customers into Low, Medium, and High risk

-> Quantify monthly revenue at risk

-> Identify the key drivers of churn

-> Enable targeted and efficient retention strategies
